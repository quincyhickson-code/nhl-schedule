Spoiler-free mode

Scores are hidden by default. Toggle Show scores in Filters to reveal them. The watched (○) button marks completed games so you can track what you've seen.

OT & SO

Games decided in overtime show an OT badge; shootout wins show SO. Both appear next to the final score when scores are visible.

My Team

Open Favorite teams, star your favorites, then tap My Team on one. The app switches to that team's colors and highlights their games.

Filters

Filter by conference (Eastern / Western) and division (Atlantic, Metropolitan, Central, Pacific). Combine with the search box for quick lookups.

Rosters & Players

Tap Roster on any game card (or click a team in Teams view) to see the current roster. Tap a player to see their profile. Star players to save them to the Players panel.

Data

Data comes from the ESPN public API and refreshes every 10 minutes. Hit Refresh to pull the latest immediately. Playoff series info (e.g. "Series tied 2-2") appears as a note on game cards.
